About SUKENBAGS™

Our innovative solution to the global plastic pollution crisis.

Made from corn starch, PLA, and PBAT, SUKENBAGS™ offers a remarkable 60% reduction in CO2 emissions compared to traditional plastics.

Free of GMO materials, Phthalate, and Bisphenol A (BPA), SUKENBAGS™ sets a new standard for eco-friendly packaging.

Environmental Responsibility

For a sustainable future and the well-being of future generations, it's crucial to make the shift towards biodegradable bags and packaging. Excessive plastic usage, particularly single-use plastic bags, has inflicted severe damage on our environment. SUKENBAGS™, with its reusable, dissolvable, and biodegradable nature, provides a preferred solution to combat plastic pollution and protect our oceans. It's time to choose responsible alternatives that prioritize our planet's health.
